A Little More About Raven:

Beyond her adorable looks, Raven has a unique personality. She's a fascinating mix of playful energy and calm companionship. Here are some quick facts about her:

Characteristic/Favorite Detail
Weight ~19-20 lbs
Breed Alaskan Klee Kai (often mistaken for a tiny Husky, she's full of spunk!)
Favorite Treats She absolutely *adores* crunchy dental sticks!
Favorite Games Chasing her squeaky tennis ball and tug-of-war with her rope toy.
Quirk The "Woo-Woo" Talker (a unique vocalization!)
Travel Enthusiast She especially loves to go for car rides!

Her Daily Life & Quirks:

Life with Raven is never dull! From her morning adventures to her unique ways of communicating, she brings joy to every moment. Here's a glimpse into her world:

  • **Morning Routine:** Our days usually begin with a brisk 30-minute walk around the local park. She loves sniffing out new scents and politely greeting other dogs.
  • **Nap Enthusiast:** Raven is a master napper! You can often find her curled up in the most comical positions, sometimes completely upside down with all four paws in the air.
  • **The "Woo-Woo" Talker:** As a Klee Kai, she's quite vocal! She has a unique "woo-woo" sound she uses when she wants attention, is excited, or simply has something important to tell me.
  • **Intuitive Companion:** Beyond her official ESA duties, she's just incredibly attuned to emotions. If I'm feeling down, she'll often bring me a toy as if to say, "Let's play and feel better!"
  • **Training Smarts:** She's a quick learner and knows commands like "sit," "stay," "come," and even a cute "high-five!"
